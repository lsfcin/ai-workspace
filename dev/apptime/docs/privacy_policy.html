AppTime — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What AppTime collects

AppTime records which apps you use, for how long, and how often you open them. This information is collected exclusively through the Android Usage Stats permission (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS), which you grant explicitly during onboarding.

2. Where your data is stored

All data is stored only on your device, in the app's private SharedPreferences storage. AppTime has no servers, no cloud sync, and makes no network connections of any kind.

3. Who can access your data

Only you. AppTime never transmits data to the developer, to third parties, or to any external service. The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no crash-reporting libraries that transmit data off-device.

4. Data retention and deletion

Usage records older than 90 days are deleted automatically each time the app starts. You can erase all recorded history at any time from Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete all data. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

5. Overlay permission

AppTime requests the Display over other apps permission (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) to show a small usage counter on screen. The overlay is purely for display — it is configured as non-touchable and cannot intercept your taps or capture any input.

6. Permissions summary

  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS — reads which apps are in the foreground to track usage time. Required. Never transmitted off-device.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW — draws the floating usage counter. Display-only; non-touchable.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — keeps the monitoring service running while you use your phone.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — restarts monitoring after reboot if it was previously active.

7. Children

AppTime is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
